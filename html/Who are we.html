Who are we

We are a young and enthusiastic company started in 2016 looking to expand the use of both Virtual reality and Augmented reality in Southern Africa.
Thanks to our partnership with Eon Reality one of the worlds fore-runners in the world of VR and AR
We have a very distinct advantage thanks to the huge library at our disposal as well as a massive network of Knowledge base to work with which makes developing for the growing market fast and easier.


Our team consists of some of the most skilled developers in the field as well as top Marketing and fully trained Sales people to help you through the entire project. Effective communication is part of our mantra to insure your upmost satisfaction.
